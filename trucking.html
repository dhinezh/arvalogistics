Trucking

Through Arva Logistics you can send your groupage shipments (LTL), full trailers (FTL) and containers quickly and reliably across India.

We always search the ideal combination of price, quality and speed to bring your shipments from A to B.

Of course, we want to make the mobility process more sustainable by keeping CO2 emissions as low as possible. This is an important factor when we select our suppliers.

Due to our years of experience with the transport of hazardous substances, you can contact Arva logistics as well for your ADR shipments.

However, depending on the destination, certain UN numbers and ADR classes are excluded. For all destinations, transport of ADR Class 1 (Explosive), 6.2 (Infectious) and 7 (Radioactive) is always excluded.
